# Privacy Policy for Balloon Battle

**Last updated: April 2026**

## Overview

Balloon Battle is a mobile arcade game developed as an independent project. This Privacy Policy explains how the game handles your information.

The short version: **Balloon Battle does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.** Everything stays on your device.

---

## Information We Collect

**We collect no personal information.**

Balloon Battle does not collect, store, transmit, or share:

- Your name, email address, or any contact information
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Advertising identifiers
- Financial information
- Health or biometric data
- Any other personal information

---

## Local Storage

Balloon Battle saves the following data **locally on your device only**, using your device's built-in storage:

- Your game progress (levels completed, stars earned, high scores)
- Your character customisation choices (skin tone, hair colour, outfit)
- Your app settings (sound on/off, visual quality preference, difficulty)
- Your daily challenge results and login streak count

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, and we have no access to it.

---

## Multiplayer

Balloon Battle includes an optional local network multiplayer mode. When using multiplayer:

- Your device connects directly to a local relay server on your network
- Only gameplay data (position, actions, scores) is transmitted during an active session
- No data is stored after the session ends
- No account or login is required
- No personal information is transmitted

---

## Third-Party Services

Balloon Battle does **not** use:

- Advertising networks
- Analytics services (e.g. Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude)
- Crash reporting services
- Social media SDKs
- Any third-party SDKs that collect data

All fonts used in the game are bundled locally within the app and do not make any external network requests.

---

## Children's Privacy

Balloon Battle is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no personal information from anyone, there are no special provisions required for users under the age of 13 (COPPA) or under the age of 16 (GDPR). We do not knowingly or unknowingly collect data from children or adults.

---

## Data Retention

Because no data is transmitted off your device, we hold no data to retain or delete. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored game data is removed automatically by your operating system.

If you wish to clear your saved progress while keeping the app installed, you can use the **Reset Progress** option in the game's Settings screen.
