Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026
The short version Balloon Battle does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.
✓ No data collected

Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. Balloon Battle does not collect, store, transmit, or share:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Financial information
  • Health or biometric data
  • Any other personal information

Local Storage

Balloon Battle saves the following data locally on your device only, using your device's built-in storage:

  • Your game progress — levels completed, stars earned, high scores
  • Your character customisation choices — skin tone, hair colour, outfit
  • Your app settings — sound, visual quality, difficulty
  • Your daily challenge results and login streak

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, and we have no access to it.

Multiplayer

Balloon Battle includes an optional local network multiplayer mode. When using multiplayer:

  • Your device connects directly to a local relay server on your network
  • Only gameplay data — position, actions, scores — is transmitted during a session
  • No data is stored after the session ends
  • No account or login is required
  • No personal information is transmitted

Third-Party Services

Balloon Battle does not use advertising networks, analytics services, crash reporting, social media SDKs, or any third-party SDKs that collect data. All fonts are bundled locally and make no external network requests.

Children's Privacy

Balloon Battle is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no personal information from anyone, there are no special provisions required for users under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR). We do not knowingly or unknowingly collect data from children or adults.

Deleting Your Data

Because no data is transmitted off your device, we hold nothing to delete. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data automatically. You can also use the Reset Progress option in the game's Settings screen to clear saved progress while keeping the app installed.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future update, the updated version will be posted at this URL and the "Last updated" date will be revised. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository or contact the developer directly through GitHub.
