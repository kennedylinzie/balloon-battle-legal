Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2026
The short version Balloon Battle stores your progress locally on your device. We use Google AdMob to show ads and Google Play for in-app purchases. We do not run our own servers or collect personal data ourselves.
✓ Local storage only

Data We Store

Balloon Battle saves the following data locally on your device only, using your device's built-in storage:

  • Your game progress — levels completed, stars earned, high scores
  • Your character customisation choices — skin tone, hair colour, outfit, balloon skin
  • Your app settings — sound, visual quality
  • Your daily challenge results and login streak
  • Coins earned and cosmetics unlocked

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server operated by us.

Third-Party Services

Balloon Battle uses the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy.

Google AdMob
We use Google AdMob to display rewarded video ads. AdMob may collect and use your device's advertising ID to serve ads. You can opt out of personalised ads in your device settings under Privacy → Ads. AdMob's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Google Play Billing (In-App Purchases)
Optional coin packs can be purchased through Google Play. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google. We receive only a confirmation that a purchase was completed — we never see or store your payment details. Google Play's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

RevenueCat
We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchase state. RevenueCat may store a non-personal app user ID and purchase history to verify entitlements. RevenueCat's privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

Advertising ID

Google AdMob uses your device's Advertising ID (GAID on Android) to serve and measure ads. This is a resettable identifier controlled by you. To reset it or opt out of personalised advertising, go to your device Settings → Privacy → Ads.

Children's Privacy

Balloon Battle is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app uses Google AdMob, which applies its own child-directed treatment settings. If you believe a child has been affected in a way not covered here, please contact us using the details below.

Deleting Your Data

Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored game data automatically. You can also use the Reset Progress option in the game's Settings screen to clear saved progress while keeping the app installed.

For data held by Google (AdMob, Play) or RevenueCat, refer to their respective privacy policies and account tools.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future update, the updated version will be posted at this URL and the "Last updated" date will be revised. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
